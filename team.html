01. MISSION & ETHOS

WHY WE BUILD

PHILOSOPHY

Tacitus.me is building a world-class Conflict Intelligence & Resolution Technology Suite. We believe conflict is not a pathology to be cured, but an information problem to be solved.

Our systems help families, boards, ministries, and mediators see structure where there is noise—mapping polarization, diagnosing constraints, and revealing resolution pathways before crises harden.

Core Thesis: AI should strengthen deliberation, widen common ground, and protect conscience—not accelerate division.

>> PROTOCOLS OF ENGAGEMENT

  • Conflict is Information Asymmetry Friction peaks where structure is hidden. We resolve the information gap that prevents actors from seeing the ZOPA (Zone of Possible Agreement).
  • Concordia Discors "Harmony in Discord." Unity is not uniformity. We engineer systems that sustain deep pluralism without collapsing into violence or silence.
  • Neutrality as Architecture The engine has no opinion. It provides the structural geometry required for diverse actors to navigate friction without coercion.
  • Agency Over Automation We do not automate decisions. We automate the intelligence needed to make them, preserving human agency and moral responsibility.
  • Privacy as Sovereignty Conflict data is toxic waste if leaked. Our architecture is air-gapped and local-first by design to protect the negotiation space.
