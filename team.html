OUR PURPOSE

PRE-SEED

Tacitus.me is building a world-class Conflict Intelligence & Resolution Technology Suite — a system designed to give clarity to anyone facing a dispute: executives, mediators, teams, families, negotiators, policymakers, and institutions. Our mission is simple and radical:

Unlock common ground at scale. Reveal structure where others see chaos. Use AI not to inflame division, but to improve deliberation, harmony, and early detection of conflict precursors like polarization. Tacitus is built for a world where noise is high, trust is low, and decisions cannot wait.
