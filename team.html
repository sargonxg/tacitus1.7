01. OUR PURPOSE

WHY WE BUILD

PRE-SEED

Tacitus.me is building a world-class Conflict Intelligence & Resolution Technology Suite. We believe conflict is information asymmetry, and that clarity should be a public good.

Our systems help families, boards, ministries, and mediators see structure where there is noise—mapping polarization, diagnosing constraints, and revealing resolution pathways before crises harden.

Mission: AI should strengthen deliberation, widen common ground, and protect conscience—not accelerate division.
