00. WHO WE ARE

OUR PURPOSE

PRE-SEED

Tacitus.me is building a world-class Conflict Intelligence & Resolution Technology Suite — tools designed to help people understand conflict, prevent escalation, and uncover common ground.

We believe conflict is information asymmetry and that conflict clarity should be for everyone. Our systems help families, executives, mediators, negotiators, and institutions see structure where there is noise — mapping polarization, diagnosing constraints, and revealing resolution pathways before crises harden.

We are early, but our mission is already clear: AI should strengthen deliberation, widen common ground, protect freedom and conscience — not accelerate division.
