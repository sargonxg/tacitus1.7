01. WHO WE ARE

>> Why We Are Building Tacitus

Tacitus grew out of work inside multilateral institutions, boardrooms, and live conflicts, where decisions were being made in fog: crucial information trapped in inboxes, side-channels, and draft texts that never made it into a shared picture.

We are a small team at the intersection of conflict resolution, diplomacy, and applied AI. The goal is simple: give leaders clarity in conflict — a structured view of actors, interests, constraints, and options — without flattening the moral and political stakes that make these decisions hard.
